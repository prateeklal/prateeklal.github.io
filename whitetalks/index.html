ABOUT WHITETALKS

White Talks is an exclusive online forum for doctors with privacy, anonymity and confidentiality guaranteed. It is a platform that provides anonymity if one desires so that legitimate concerns pertaining to areas of medical practice may be aired or a different perspective on a complex case may be sought without violating patient or doctor confidentiality.

WHAT MAKES WHITETALKS AN UNIQUE FORUM

We maintain absolute confidentiality or anonymity, a prerequisite to good medical ethics and safety from legal repercussions. Patient trust and Doctor’s trust is never to be violated.

WHAT MAKES WHITETALKS AN UNIQUE FORUM

We are a medical resource center which can be constantly tapped into to get answers without delay. The knowledge bank is constantly augmented and updated with knowledge from doctors and reputed institutions globally.

WHAT MAKES WHITETALKS AN UNIQUE FORUM

We facilitate collaborative effort in tackling complicated medical cases or a tricky diagnosis. Doctors would be able to share such cases with their peers and share their expertise and experience in dealing with such cases.

WHAT MAKES WHITETALKS AN UNIQUE FORUM

We make possible free and frank dialogue without fearing adverse career ramifications. You can express your thoughts, apprehensions, frustrations without the fear of it coming back to haunt you.

WHAT MAKES WHITETALKS AN UNIQUE FORUM

You may log in and log off at your convenience and discretion so that your routine is not affected and at the same time, you are able to use White Talks around your routine to your advantage.
