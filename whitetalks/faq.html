FAQS

  1. What is White Talks’ mission?
    2.  White Talks’ mission is to transform the medical world through rapid dissemination of medical information across boundaries and meaningful collaboration between doctors and the pharmaceutical industry.
  2. How does White Talks enhance doctors’ proficiency?

    3. White Talks enhances medical proficiency through the following means:

    • A platform for doctors to interact and discuss freely
    • Collaborative treatment via mobile technology
    • Solutions to tough cases in a short span of time
    • A Comprehensive Database for Drugs and Drug Information
  3. Is it safe for doctors to discuss medical and professional issues on White Talks?
    4. Absolutely! Doctor-patient confidentiality is maintained as doctors and patients are anonymous when case studies take place. Other professional issues that are discussed are confidential and will not be leaked out.
  4. How is White Talks different from other forums?
    5. White Talks is not just an inanimate point of reference but a platform where actual discussions, interactions and crowd sourcing take place.
  5. Is anonymous participation possible in White Talks’ discussions?
    6. Yes it is. This is in order to protect identities of doctors when discussing professional issues such misdiagnosis, fatigue, frustration at the workplace, etc, which may have serious repercussions.
  6. Is the verification process fool proof as doctors may choose to remain anonymous on White Talks?
    7. The verification process is a many tiered process and 100% fool proof.
