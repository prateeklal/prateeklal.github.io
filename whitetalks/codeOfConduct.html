Code Of Conduct

As a doctor, you are an integral part of the discussions and interactions on www.whitetalks.com (“Site”). A certain code of conduct should be adhered to ensure optimal use of this Site for dissemination of information and a healthy discussion.

Respect different opinions and perspectives just as you would in a dialogue face-to-face. Etiquette has to be maintained even when faced with an opinion with which you may not agree.

Since this Site is for you to consult, discuss and benefit from rapidly disseminated medical information, it is recommended to upload relevant photos of patient cases for which you are seeking advice or information. However, ensure that you have removed any identifying information before uploading as patient confidentiality is of paramount importance.

Moderate your tone, post or comment to your peers on this forum. Don’t say what you wouldn’t say to them in person. However, if it is found that you are not capable of moderation, then we will moderate your account for you. Repeat instances of moderation will result in your account being deactivated.

Please report intemperate online behaviour, verbal abuse or threats and other violations of the code of conduct to admin@whitetalks.com. We adhere to a ‘zero tolerance’ policy to attacks based on social strata, racial, ethnic and religious lines or gender and sexual orientation. No name calling, profanity, threats-direct or indirect will be tolerated on this Site.

Refrain from posting anything that needs to be reported to the law enforcement agencies.

Since this Site provides for anonymity, do not reveal information about another member other than what the member has chosen to divulge about him/her.

If you want to express yourself, you can always start a new post instead of taking over someone else’s thread of conversation.

Stalking or any kind of sexual harassment will be reported to the law enforcement agencies and lead to automatic deactivation of account.

Commercial or fundraising efforts are not encouraged on this Site.

You are required to comply with the provisions of the Indian Medical Council (Professional conduct, Etiquette and Ethics) Regulations, 2002 and other applicable laws at all times.

Your account will be immediately deactivated on sharing your password with others (current member/past member/outsider), impersonation of members or White Talks staff or allowing access to a banned member.

The decision to deactivate an account is taken after review of the track record of the member concerned, i.e., the number of warnings issued and suspensions.

Further queries on this Code of Conduct may be sent to admin@whitetalks.com.
