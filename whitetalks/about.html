About Us


What is WhiteTalks all about?

WhiteTalks is an exclusive online global community of doctors. It is a medical social network wherein doctors can share, discuss, brainstorm, learn, and connect globally with peers and experts.

What is exclusive about WhiteTalks?

WhiteTalks is an exclusive online forum for doctors with privacy, anonymity and confidentiality guaranteed. It is a platform that provides anonymity if one desires so that legitimate concerns pertaining to areas of medical practice may be aired or a different perspective on a complex case may be sought without violating patient or doctor confidentiality. A registered user has the advantage of exploring career opportunities, participating in surveys by pharmaceutical companies that have an impact on drug discovery, drug availability, the latest breakthrough in medicine or research etc.

Our members are registered doctors, whose credentials are verified, from all over the world

We provide a virtual forum for doctors to interact, confer, research and share thereby creating a discussion platform for a strong global community of doctors. Doctors are no longer restricted by the narrow confines of the local medical fraternity or geographical boundaries. The access to a global community results in best medical practices and, quick updating on the latest practices, discoveries and innovations thereby providing the best medical care and developing the best medical practices.

What makes WhiteTalks a unique forum?

  • We maintain absolute confidentiality or anonymity, a prerequisite to good medical ethics and safety from legal repercussions. Patient trust and Doctor’s trust is never to be violated.
  • We are a medical resource center which can be constantly tapped into to get answers without delay. The knowledge bank is constantly augmented and updated with knowledge from doctors and reputed institutions globally.
  • We facilitate collaborative effort in tackling complicated medical cases or a tricky diagnosis. Doctors would be able to share such cases with their peers and share their expertise and experience in dealing with such cases.
  • We make possible free and frank dialogue without fearing adverse career ramifications. You can express your thoughts, apprehensions, frustrations without the fear of it coming back to haunt you.
  • You may log in and log off at your convenience and discretion so that your routine is not affected and at the same time, you are able to use White Talks around your routine to your advantage.
  • Doctors would be able to access detailed and comprehensive information about drugs, drug interactions, and adverse events.

WhiteTalks Community

Be part of an exclusive medical community! Once you sign up, you will be assigned to the department of your specialization. For example, an orthopedic doctor will be assigned to the orthopedic department. However, s/he is not restricted to interacting only within this department but can always access other departments.
On signing up, the benefits are manifold as one can:
  • upload case studies
  • upload X-rays and other reports
  • participate in surveys posted by pharmaceutical companies
  • participate in polls by pharmaceutical companies
  • initiate a peer-to-peer poll
  • start a blog or forum for discussion
Verifications are thorough and detailed in order to make sure that the community stays exclusive to the medical fraternity
