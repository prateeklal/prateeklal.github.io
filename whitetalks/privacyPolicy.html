WHITETALKS PRIVACY POLICY


SV Health Nova Pvt. Ltd. marketed and traded under the name of WhiteTalks (“WT”) respects your right to privacy and we strive to be transparent at all times about the treatment of your information. WT is the author and publisher of the internet resource www.whitetalks.com (“Site”) on the World Wide Web. This Privacy Policy (“Privacy Policy”) discloses the privacy practices for WT with regard to your use of the Site. Our Privacy Policy In the course of providing you a high quality WT experience and through your interactions with us, WT collects information about you and about your use of our Site. Where WT operates, we comply with applicable privacy and data protection laws. This Privacy Policy is governed amongst other things, by Section 43A of the Information Technology Act, 2000, Regulation 4 of the Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Information) Rules, 2011 (“SPI Rules”) and Regulation 3(1) of the Information Technology (Intermediaries Guidelines) Rules, 2011 regarding the collection, use and retention of personal information in which WT does business.

  1. Information we collect
    2. When you visit WT, some information is automatically collected. For example, when you visit our Site, your computer's operating system, Internet Protocol Address (“IP”), access times, browser type, language and the website you visited before our site are logged automatically. We also collect information about your usage and activity on WT. For more information about this practice please refer to the “How we use and share your information” section below. We collect your email when you opt-in to “Sign up for the latest from WT” or when you contact us, so that we could reply to you. We also collect information that you may provide us with when contacting us such as:
    1. Personal Identifiable Information (“PII”) which includes first and last names, email address, telephone number(s).
    2. Business contact information, such as company name, job title, and department.
    3. Information on your employer or the company you represent.
  2. How we use and share your information
    3. We will use your email address (and other information you may have chosen to share with us) to communicate with you regarding important WT account management news and other information as it becomes available.
    1. Cookies
      2. A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. A cookie cannot read data off your hard disk or read cookie files created by other sites. Use of a cookie is in no way linked to any PII while on the Site. For instance, by setting a cookie on your browser, you would not have to log in a password more than once, thereby saving time while on the Site. You can choose whether to accept cookies by changing the settings of your browser. You can reset your browser to refuse all cookies or allow your browser to show you when a cookie is being sent. If you reject the cookies on the Site, you may still be able to use the Site but it shall be limited to certain minimal functionality. One drawback to this is that the user may be limited in some areas of our Site or limited to certain functions of the Site.
    2. Sale of Assets, Merger, Acquisition or Bankruptcy
      3. Information collected from you may be transferred to a third party as a result of a sale of assets, acquisition, merger or bankruptcy involving WT or for any other reason.
    3. Log Files
      4. WT may use IP and application usage information to align the product offering with changes in the usage patterns, administer the Site and gather broad demographic information for aggregate use.
    4. Spam
      5. WT maintains a strict “No-Spam” policy, which means that WT does not intend to sell, rent or otherwise give your e-mail address to a third party without your consent.
    5. Legal Requests
      6. WT takes your right to privacy seriously and has taken measures to safeguard and respect your personal information. WT will only disclose your personal information in the event it is required to do so by law, rule, regulation, law enforcement or to any governmental official, legal authority or similar requirements.

    4. We are committed to protecting the security of your information. We use a variety of industry-standard security technologies and procedures designed to help protect your information from unauthorized access, use or disclosure. Despite these measures, you should know that we cannot fully eliminate security risks associated with information and mistakes may happen.

Such information may also be converted to physical form from time to time. WT takes all necessary precautions to protect your personal information both online and off-line, and implements reasonable security practices and measures including certain managerial, technical, operational and physical security control measures that commensurate with respect to the information being collected and the nature of WT’s business.
