About Falcon

Established in 1995, Falcon Vintners offers an extensive range of fine wines from young and mature vintages. Our speciality are the fine wine of Italy and France but we also cover other parts of Europe and the New World.

Buy

We specialise in highly sought-after, hard-to-find and older vintages but also a good range of everyday drinking wines. Most of our stock is available for immediate delivery.

Sell

If you’re looking to sell we’ll aim to get the best price for you and charge competitive commission rates.

Store

All our wines are stored at the temperature controlled warehouse, Vinotheque in Burton-on-Trent. We can store wines on your behalf in a sub-account at very competitive rates.
